Measure a rim — ERD

ERD = S₁ + S₂ + G — the straight line from one flush point through the centre to the other.

How to take the measurements
  1. Take two spokes you don't mind sacrificing and two nipples from your build.
  2. Thread a nipple onto each until the spoke end is flush with the bottom of the screwdriver slot.
  3. Measure each from the inside of the elbow to that flush point: S₁ and S₂.
  4. Drop both into two directly-opposite spoke holes, nipples seated, spokes pointing toward the centre.
  5. Measure the gap between the two elbows across the middle: G (negative if they overlap).

Measure a hub — flange dimensions

Rear and disc hubs are asymmetric — enter left and right separately.

How to take the measurements
  1. Flange PCD (the spoke-hole circle): measure centre-to-centre across two opposite holes, or across the flange and subtract a hole diameter.
  2. Centre-to-flange (left and right): halve the over-locknut width to find the centre plane, then measure from a locknut face to each flange and subtract.
